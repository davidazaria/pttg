how does the game work?

this is a fun, interactive game testing your ability to spot a real or fake president donald trump tweet. as we know, president trump is a prolific tweeter. so much so that his tweets have the ability to shape foreign affairs, capital markets, the media zeitgeist, and even sports policy. with that, president trump as offered us a rare glimpse into the psyche of a world leader. part of this exercise is to only have a good time guessing whether a tweet was produced by president trump or not, but additionally highlighting our own biases in determining true from fake.
