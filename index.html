how does the game work?

this is a fun, interactive game testing your ability to spot a real or fake president donald trump tweet. as we know, president trump is a prolific tweeter, so much so that his tweets have the ability to shape foreign affairs, capital markets, the media zeitgeist, and even sporting events. its content has spanned almost topic imaginable, making a game like this one easy to produce. and with all that, president trump as offered us a rare glimpse into the psyche of a world leader. while this game is meant to be non-political, it does offer the player some insights into their ability to discern true from false given any pre-existing biases towards the president's twitter feed.
